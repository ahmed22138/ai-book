# Welcome to Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics

## Embodied Intelligence in the Real World

Welcome to an interactive, AI-native textbook on Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics. This comprehensive course bridges theoretical concepts with practical robotics applications, featuring 12 interactive lessons, AI-powered learning tools, and cutting-edge content.

### Course Overview

This textbook covers four core modules spanning 12 weeks of learning:

1. **Introduction to Embodied AI** (Weeks 1-3)
   - Embodied AI fundamentals and philosophy
   - Robot anatomy and sensing systems
   - Basic control systems

2. **Perception & Sensing** (Weeks 4-6)
   - Computer vision fundamentals
   - LiDAR and depth sensing
   - Sensor fusion techniques

3. **Control & Actuation** (Weeks 7-9)
   - Kinematics and inverse kinematics
   - Motion planning algorithms
   - Real-time control systems

4. **Multi-Robot Integration** (Weeks 10-12)
   - Multi-robot coordination systems
   - Human-robot collaboration
   - Deployment and scaling

### What You'll Learn

- **Foundational Concepts**: Understand the principles of embodied intelligence and how robots perceive, plan, and act in the physical world
- **Practical Skills**: Apply ROS 2, Python, and C++ to real robotics problems
- **Hands-On Experience**: Work with multiple hardware options (workstations, edge kits, physical robots, simulation, budget platforms)
- **Advanced Topics**: Multi-robot systems, human-robot interaction, and production deployment

### Features

- **AI-Powered Learning**: Use our RAG chatbot to ask questions about any lesson content with instant, sourced answers
- **Personalized Content**: Adapt lesson complexity based on your background and expertise level
- **Multi-Language Support**: Read lessons in Urdu or other languages
- **Interactive Agents**: Generate ROS 2 code, create assessments, and produce diagrams with AI subagents
- **Capstone Project**: Integrate all concepts into a comprehensive capstone project

### Prerequisites

- Basic programming experience (Python or C++)
- Linear algebra and calculus fundamentals
- Curiosity about robotics and AI!
